## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

### Q: What is Kreta?

A: Kreta is a native SwiftUI iOS app designed to help you track Indonesian trains in real-time.

### Q: Is the app free to use?

A: Yes, Kreta is free to download and use.

### Q: How do I report a bug?

A: If you encounter any issues, please email us at the address above with a description of the problem and any relevant screenshots.

### Q: Does the app work offline?

A: While some features may be available offline, an active internet connection is required for real-time train tracking.
